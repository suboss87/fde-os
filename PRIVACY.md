# Privacy Policy

FDEOS is a local plugin. It collects no data, has no backend, and makes no network requests of its own.

## What FDEOS does

- Reads and writes files inside the `.fde/` directory in your project
- Instructs your AI assistant (Claude or equivalent) how to behave during an engagement
- All context -- trust profiles, stakeholder maps, codebase notes, decisions -- stays on your machine

## What FDEOS does not do

- No telemetry
- No analytics
- No data sent to any server
- No user accounts or registration
- No third-party integrations

## Your .fde/ directory

The `.fde/` directory may contain sensitive customer information. It is your responsibility to add `.fde/` to your `.gitignore` and ensure it is not committed or shared. FDEOS does not protect this data on your behalf -- it only creates it.

## AI assistant data handling

FDEOS instructs your AI assistant how to behave. What your AI assistant does with the content you share is governed by the privacy policy of that assistant (e.g. Anthropic's privacy policy for Claude). FDEOS has no control over and no visibility into that data.

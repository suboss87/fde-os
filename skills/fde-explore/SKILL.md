# FDE Explore – First Contact

## Purpose
Find the real problem by doing messy, manual, high-touch work. The FDE mantra: "Do things that don't scale, at scale." This skill prevents premature automation.

## When Activated
- First engagement with a customer.
- You have no proven playbook for the domain.
- User says "we don't know where to start."

## Protocol
1. **Manual Walkthrough** – perform the customer's task manually, using their existing tools, even if it's copy-pasting spreadsheets.
2. **Identify Friction** – what made you slow? What felt stupid? That's where the real problem lives.
3. **Build a Throwaway** – create the simplest possible script or UI that solves 5% of the problem. No error handling, no tests. Just enough to show.
4. **Demo Immediately** – show the throwaway and ask: "Is this even close?"
5. **Listen for Latent Need** – they'll say "no, but…" – that's gold.
6. **Iterate or Discard** – mutilate the prototype or throw it away. Don't polish.

## Output
A stream of notes: what worked, what surprised you, what the customer said.

## Iron Rule
**Never skip exploration. If you catch yourself writing clean code before showing a rough demo, stop.**

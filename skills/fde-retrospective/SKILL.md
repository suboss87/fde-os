# FDE Retrospective - Learning Engine

## Purpose
Turn every engagement into an organisational asset. Feed patterns back into the product and this skill pack.

## When Activated
- After shipping (end of `fde-master` Phase 7).
- After a significant incident or bug fix.

## Retrospective Protocol
1. **Decisions Log**: what we chose, why, alternatives.
2. **Mistakes Made**: what went wrong, could it have been prevented? (Blame-free.)
3. **Surprises**: unexpected events, good or bad.
4. **Architecture Evolution**: update `docs/fde/codebase-context.md`.
5. **Upstream Pattern Extraction**: what can be fed back to product teams? (Write a summary.)
6. **Skill Improvement**: should any FDEOS skill be updated? Log suggestion.

## Deliverable
`docs/fde/retrospectives/YYYY-MM-DD-engagement-name.md`

## Iron Rule
**A retrospective is not a punishment. It's an investment in the next session's intelligence.**
